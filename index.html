About O nas

Sisi Dog Couture is a small independent brand creating handmade clothing and accessories for pets. Each piece is crafted from high-quality materials with a focus on comfort, aesthetics, and your pet’s unique personality. I believe in slow fashion, uniqueness, and products made with heart.
